# Cranamp Demo Music Prompts

Use these prompts with ACE-Step 1.5 text-to-music generation only.

Rules:

- Do not upload reference audio.
- Do not request vocals.
- Do not use artist names.
- Do not use "in the style of ..." prompts.
- Do not use copyrighted samples or third-party music.
- Keep generated tracks short and instrumental.
- Prefer WAV or FLAC raw export, then convert reviewed files to `.mp3`.
