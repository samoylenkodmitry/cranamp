# Demo Music Licensing

The files in this directory are demo/test audio assets for Cranamp.

They are intentionally separated from the source-code license. Do not assume Cranamp's source-code license applies to generated audio files unless a specific asset has its own documented license or release note.

Generated tracks are created with ACE-Step 1.5.

Generation constraints:

- instrumental only
- no vocals
- no artist names in prompts
- no "in the style of ..." prompts
- no uploaded reference audio
- no samples from third-party music
- intended as generated demo/test assets for the Cranamp app

ACE-Step 1.5 project:
https://github.com/ace-step/ACE-Step-1.5

ACE-Step 1.5 model:
https://huggingface.co/ACE-Step/Ace-Step1.5

ACE-Step 1.5 is documented by its project as MIT licensed, and its disclaimer asks users to verify originality, disclose AI involvement, and avoid protected styles or materials. Keep those constraints in mind when generating, reviewing, and publishing demo audio.
